CONTROL A CHAOTIC DUO

Move Smash with the left stick, throw Grab with the right.
Simple controls that are easy to pick up, and criminally satisfying to master as you smash and grab your way to the top!

Smash your way through breakable environments where destruction is rewarded.

Tethered by a rope, Smash can launch Grab to reel enemies in, seize valuables, and grapple through the environment.
