Studio Info

SLAP-BANG! Digital
Based in Belfast, Northern Ireland

Website: slapbangdigital.com
Email: slapbangdigital@gmail.com

SLAP-BANG! Digital is a Belfast-based independent game studio founded in October 2024 by Mark Skelton. The studio was established with a mission to create bold, visually striking games that are easy to pick up and play, but packed with personality and challenge.

At the end of 2024, SLAP-BANG! Digital received prototype funding from NI Screen, which enabled the formation of a small but focused team. Over the following months, the team developed a vertical slice for Smash 'N Grab, the studio’s debut project.
